Bear-Human Conflicts!

This time, the brown bear killed two yaks ---- located in Yushu Qinghai China

Process 过程

On March 20, the forest police brigade of the Yushu Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture Public Security Bureau in Qinghai Province issued a notice saying that herdsmen found an adult wild brown bear in Zhenqin Qichun Munagou, Chengduo County, and the brown bear bit two yaks to death. After the police arrived at the scene, the brown bear was still waiting near the dead yak and refused to leave. After more than four hours of "man-bear game", the brown bear finally left.

Brown bears have repeatedly bitten herders, attacked livestock, and even killed local people in Yushu, Qinghai Province. Local police have repeatedly issued "brown bear warnings" to remind local farmers and herdsmen to raise awareness and safety awareness to protect personal life and property safety.

Reason 原因

Local police said that "the weather on the plateau was still cold in March, and the brown bears had difficulty finding food outside, which led them to attack the yaks."

Bear Attack!

A black bear attack occurred in Jiangyou, Sichuan Province

Process 过程

On May 17, a black bear attack occurred in Jiangyou, Sichuan Province, resulting in three deaths, among which Zhu Mingcai, Group 8 leader of Shenshui Village in Majjiao Town, was attacked and killed by a black bear during the search and rescue process at night.

On the morning of May 19th, in the early morning of May 18, a black bear suddenly escaped from a chase car and was shot dead by special police.

On May 19, the Jiangyou Municipal Bureau of Natural Resources once again organized a hunting team of more than 10 people with 12 hunting dogs into the mountain to continue searching for the black bear, but no trace was found as of the end of the search. On May 20, search teams will continue to search wooded areas and intersections where bears may be present.

Result 结果

On the afternoon of the same day, Jiangyou City Bureau of Natural Resources with a group of team members walked down from the mountain, he introduced, in order to maintain the safety of people's lives, 18, 19, hunting teams with hunting dogs on Ma Jiao Town Shenshui village people live near the forest area search, as of now (19 search has stopped), no trace of the bear was found.

On May 20, search teams will continue to search wooded areas and intersections where bears may be present. If the bear is found, the hunting party will drive the bear to the forest area. The fierce bear who is not afraid of people attacking people will be shot to prevent the bear from hurting people again.

Expert Zhang Yong Said 专家张勇观点

  1. Animals have a safe distance, beyond the safe distance, wild animals are afraid of people.
  2. The presence of black bears reflects that the local ecological environment protection has made achievements, because black bears are at the top of the food chain, and the existence of black bears indicates that the next chain of wild animals is also increasing.
  3. The ultimate purpose of environmental protection is to protect people, protect the scientific environment, so that wild animals can survive in the natural environment and grow freely in a free environment, and human beings should not interfere too much.

How Does China Do?

Chinese Policy and Wildlife Protection Measures

China 中国法律

《The Civil Code of the People's Republic of China》: Article 251: Wildlife resources that are owned by the State as stipulated by law shall be owned by the State. -- The State compensates individual losses

The Government 政府

Qinghai Provincial Government: With the expansion of wild animal populations in Qinghai Province in recent years, conflicts between humans and bears have occurred frequently in remote pastoral areas, seriously affecting livestock production and disrupting the lives of farmers and herdsmen. Qinghai Province "horse racing system" science and technology project "human-bear conflict early warning and prevention technology launched.

Horse Racing 赛马制

"Horse racing" is a method that addresses technical difficulties. Parallel support for multiple teams of different technical routes, compare and select the "best route." Based on publishing the list (guide), publicly unveiling the list (declaration), and expert demonstration, the organization, and implementation of projects that are first approved and then focused on by two or more reporting units in the same list.

Investigation 调查

According to statistics, from 2012 to 2017, 20 incidents of brown bear attacks occurred in Yushu Prefecture, killing 5 people, disabling 6 people, and injuring 11 people, and 570 incidents of brown bear disturbing people and damaging objects occurred, showing a rapid growth trend. At present, all the incidents of human-animal conflict causing personal injury and house damage are caused by brown bears, but there are no effective preventive measures for herders at this stage.

Organization name: The 12th Member Congress and symposium of Zhejiang Zoological Society

Research: In May 2013, we surveyed the Tibetan-brown bear conflict in Yushu Prefecture: and interviewed 29 grassroots herders. The survey shows that 62.1% of the people think that the conflict between locals and Tibetan brown bears is serious: the nomadic period from June to September is the highest incidence of human-bear conflict, mainly manifested in the destruction of house components, eating food stored in the house and causing personal casualties.

In Dangqu Village, the annual economic loss caused by Tibetan brown bears reached 796,800 yuan, accounting for 80.7% of the annual total income, with an average of 968 yuan per person per year. An average of 56.9% of the annual income of the whole surveyed region was lost due to the behavior of wild animals such as Tibetan brown bears, and 72.4% of people believed that the government should provide moderate compensation. The survey shows that the existing bear prevention methods and measures are not effective.

Suggestion 建议

  1. Setting up joint defense teams, evacuating homes and villages, rebuilding houses.
  2. Strengthening bear prevention education among residents.
  3. Setting up warning signs in areas with high incidences of bear infestation, and so on, slowing down or preventing human-bear conflicts, capturing bears that cause casualties among residents, and sending them to zoos.

Face to the Bears

Understanding Bear Behavior and Safety Guidelines

In Qinghai, Tibet, Yunnan, and other regions of China, there are many large animals, so it is easy to have human-animal conflicts. A prime example is the bear. So how do we avoid this kind of conflict?

The First Thing to Understand is the Characteristics of Bears 了解熊的特征

Basics 基础知识: Bears are very common and aggressive animals. The brown bear is one of the largest carnivorous mammals on land, with a body length of 1-2.8 meters, a tail length of 6.5-2.1 centimeters, and a weight of 80-600 kilograms. Brown bears in China generally prefer to stay in the jungle to hide during the day.

Territory 领地意识: Brown bears have a very important characteristic, their territorial sense is very strong, Each brown bear has its land (home), and they do not tolerate any species or humans entering it, because it will be considered provocative.

Hibernation 冬眠: Brown bears have the habit of hibernating, starting in late October or early November, until March-April next year. To accumulate the approximately 50 kg of fat needed during hibernation, 400-600 kg of berries and other foods must be eaten in the autumn.

Senses 感官: Brown bears have a very sensitive sense of smell and can smell a dead body for more than three kilometers. But their eyesight is very poor.

Knowing the Characteristics of Brown Bears, How Can We Avoid Conflict? 如何避免冲突？

Reasons ⚠️⚠️⚠️ 冲突原因

  1. Territorial Awareness 领地意识: Pay attention! Bears will never tolerate human intrusion into their territory e.g. In Tibet, local herdsmen have conflicts with brown bears because the herdsmen move their animals into the territory of these bears and are attacked by them.
  2. Food 食物: Bears generally do not actively attack humans. But if the bear can't find food, especially when winter is coming. They will actively attack human captive poultry as a last resort. After all, nothing tastes better than beef, and of course, to stay alive.

Methods ❗️❗️❗️ 避免冲突的方法

  1. Stay calm and don't run away 保持冷静，不要逃跑: Especially if you accidentally enter bear territory. Bear vision is very poor, if it is far away, it means that it will most likely not find you. At this point, you can leave slowly. Don't run away, it will attract its attention! (The bear's speed is 56 km/h.)
  2. Do not approach bear cubs 不要接近熊崽: If you see bear cubs, stay away from them, as the mother will attack you in a desperate attempt to protect her young.
  3. Show the bear that you are not a threat 向熊表明你没有威胁: If the bear notices you, raise your hands and slowly step back while speaking loudly to show the bear that you are not a threat.
