网站简介 Introduction

欢迎访问"人熊冲突与共存"网站！本站建立初衷在于希望人们注意并重视起人与动物之间的生存和利益冲突， 其中人与熊之间是典型的例子。我们希望人与动物可以和谐共处，共同发展。

Glad to visit the "Human-Bear Conflict and Coexistence" website! The original intention is to hope that people pay attention to the survival and conflict of interest between humans and animals, of which the typical example is between humans and bears. We hope that humans and animals can coexist harmoniously and develop together.
